Team Objective - Map development


Welcome to Team Objective. Here we desire to create maps that you have always wanted. Below you can find out about our current project that we are working on.


Evocity Edit Progress

5% Complete

Evocity has gone through many changes since it was first started. The community has used the two maps to great much bigger and realistic scenario of roleplay. Since they creation of Evocity 2, many players have suggested more buildings, areas and much more for both maps. We have listened to the community and we are currently working on a new version of Evocity. There will be new buildings such as Police Station, Offices, Warehouses and much more. New land will be made for players to build on.


Custom Map Progress

1% Complete

At Team Objective, we have seen a lot of different styled roleplay maps. We wish to take all the positive assets from them maps and incorporate it into a custom created roleplay map designed for serious roleplay. We have also taken ideas and suggestions from members of different communities and noted them down. If you wish to contribute more, don't hesitate to email us!
