Welcome to the Evocity Edit

Here, you can find more information about this project.

Evocity has gone through many changes since it was first started. The community has used the two maps to create much bigger and realistic scenario of roleplay. Since they creation of Evocity 2, many players have suggested more buildings, areas and much more for both maps. We have listened to the community and we are currently working on a new version of Evocity. There will be new buildings such as Police Station, Offices, Warehouses and much more. New land will be made for players to build on.
